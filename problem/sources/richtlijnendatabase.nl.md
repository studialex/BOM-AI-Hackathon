# Richtlijnendatabase (Database of Medical Guidelines)

## What is this source?

The **Richtlijnendatabase** (https://richtlijnendatabase.nl/) is the central Dutch platform for **multidisciplinary clinical practice guidelines**. It brings together evidence‑based guidelines used by healthcare professionals across the Netherlands, including oncology, diagnostics, treatment, follow‑up, and supportive care.

The platform is widely used in Dutch healthcare to support **clinical decision‑making**, quality of care, and standardisation of medical practice. While many cancer‑related guidelines rely on data and expertise from organisations such as IKNL, the [Richtlijnendatabase](https://richtlijnendatabase.nl/) itself is **not maintained by IKNL**.
<br><br>

## Hackathon perspective: why this is an interesting source
From a hackathon point of view, the Richtlijnendatabase is interesting because it represents **practical clinical treatment guidelines** that could be combined with insights from IKNL sources.

Potential opportunities include:
- Connecting guidelines to real‑world data (e.g. cancer incidence, regional variation, ..)  
- Structuring unstructured guideline text into machine‑readable components

> **Hackathon note**  
> We do not prescribe or endorse a specific technical access method for this source. Participants may explore scraping, text extraction, or manual sampling, while remaining mindful of terms of use and ethical considerations.
